Snack Tennessee at Hack Tennessee 7

I had never heard of hackathons until I enrolled at NSS, and quite frankly, the thought of attending an all-weekend coding fest was a bit daunting. But I signed up, and days before Hack Tennessee, I came up with an idea. And with the encouragement from my cohortmate, Margaret Clinard, I decided that I should pitch the project in front of 500+ registered participants during the opening of this hackathon.
